Biology Virus
Outbreak Simulator

A deadly virus has been detected. As lead scientist, you have 7 days to contain the outbreak before it becomes a pandemic.

How to Play

Game Info:
• Seven days to contain the outbreak
• Two critical decisions per day
• Each choice affects your containment score
• AI generates unique virus scenarios
How to Play:
• Read the situation carefully
• Choose the best scientific response
• Learn from AI feedback after each decision
• Adapt your strategy based on results
Goal:
Maintain a containment score ≥ 60/100 by Day 7 to successfully contain the outbreak!
Learning Goal:
Master real epidemiology concepts: quarantine, contact tracing, resource allocation, and outbreak response strategies.
AI Help:
Use the chatbot in-game to ask biology questions and get real-time guidance!
